# OpenMotoDash

**OpenMotoDash** is an open-source organization dedicated to building better dashboard, navigation, and telemetry interfaces for motorcycles — as alternatives or enhancements to manufacturer-provided apps and displays.

Our goal is to give riders more control, transparency, and flexibility over the tech on their bikes: whether that's running a custom dashboard on a Raspberry Pi, replacing a stock companion app with something faster and more feature-rich, or simply understanding how the connected systems on modern motorcycles actually work.

This organization exists to consolidate related community projects under one roof, make it easier for contributors to collaborate across them, and keep the work maintained long-term.

## Projects

### 🖥️ [better-dash](https://github.com/OpenMotoDash/better-dash)
A Raspberry Pi–based custom dashboard interface, offering an alternative display and navigation experience to the stock manufacturer dash unit.

### 📱 [NorthStar](https://github.com/OpenMotoDash/NorthStar)
An Android app providing an alternative dashboard and navigation interface, communicating with the bike's onboard systems in place of the stock companion app.

### 📱 [open-dash](https://github.com/OpenMotoDash/open-dash)
Another Android app implementation of an alternative dashboard interface, offering a different take on the same core connectivity approach as NorthStar.
